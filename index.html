About

who's behind the queries

I'm a results-driven Data Analyst and MIS Reporting professional with over seven years of experience turning operational data into reports people actually use — sales performance, order tracking, target-vs-achievement, and KPI dashboards for manufacturing, healthcare, logistics, and engineering businesses.

My day-to-day runs on SQL for pulling and shaping data, Power BI and Power Query for modelling and visualisation, and Advanced Excel for the fast, flexible reporting that business teams need weekly and monthly. I care about the boring-but-critical parts too: data cleaning, validation, and building reports that stay accurate as the underlying data changes.
